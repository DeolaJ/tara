The vibe

Game night, rebuilt.

Tara is a pocket-sized game show studio. You pick the format, we handle the buzzers, lights, and scoreboard — so you can focus on what matters: mispronouncing your best friend's name on live TV.

No downloads for your players. They join by link, on any phone, any browser. You host from your laptop or TV. Tara does the rest.
